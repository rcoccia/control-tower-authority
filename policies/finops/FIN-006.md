# FIN-006: Development and test cost guardrail

> **SYNTHETIC DEMO POLICY** — This is synthetic content for a bounded public
> demonstration. It is not organizational policy or a production decision.

## Intent

Keep RelayEU development and test experiments within a visible EUR budget.

## Applicability

Applies where `environment` is `dev` or `test`.

## Required properties

<a id="fin-006-eur-100-cap"></a>
### `fin-006-eur-100-cap` — EUR 100 cap

Set the development or test budget cap to EUR 100.

<a id="fin-006-eighty-percent-alert"></a>
### `fin-006-eighty-percent-alert` — 80 percent alert

Alert the workload owner at 80 percent of the cap.

<a id="fin-006-reestimate"></a>
### `fin-006-reestimate` — Re-estimation

Re-estimate cost when topology, SKU, or region changes.

## Expected evidence

Provide the budget configuration, alert recipient, current estimate, and a
change record for every topology, SKU, or region re-estimation.

## Exceptions and waivers

This demo policy grants no exception or waiver. Record a request for owner
review; only the simulated policy-owner process may publish a future change.

## Progressive disclosure

Start with the property IDs above. See `assess-cloud-workload` in
`.github/skills/` for fact elicitation; it is advisory only.
